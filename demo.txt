Kerala is a beautiful state in India known for its stunning backwaters, lush green hills, and magnificent beaches.  The state is a popular tourist destination for its rich culture and diverse wildlife.
Explore the serene backwaters on a houseboat cruise, or hike through the tea plantations and spice gardens. Experience the vibrant culture of the local communities and enjoy the delicious Kerala cuisine.
From the stunning hill stations of Munnar and Wayanad to the serene beaches of Kovalam and Varkala, Kerala offers a myriad of experiences for every type of traveler.
